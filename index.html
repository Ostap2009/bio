BIO
Ostap Romanchenko was born on December 22, 1989 in Kyiv, Ukraine, in the family of a firefighter. His father was among the liquidators of the Chernobyl accident.

At the age of 4 he moved with his parents to the village of Shatura in the Chernihiv region. The artist spent his childhood and school years in Shatura. life in the village formed in him respect for other people's work and the desire to leave the village as soon as possible.

After school Ostap goes to study at an art school in Nizhyn. He passed the entrance exams in 2005 and entered the Faculty of Decorative and Applied Arts. Among the teachers were Baradulina OM, Koshel OI, Petrenko YM and Panchenko OI. The school forms a view of the world and the profession. At this time Ostap Romanchenko admires the work of Salvador Dali and Pablo Picasso. After graduating from school in 2009 I will visit the Plein Air of Young Artists in Gurzuf.

From 2009 to 2014 he studied at the Nizhyn State University named after MV Gogol at the Faculty of History. The author works in many techniques and styles. Current idols include Francis Bacon and Lucien Freud.

Now lives and works in Nizhyn.
